About Us

At SLIET, we are dedicated to nurturing talent and guiding students through their academic and career journeys. Our mentorship platform is designed to create new opportunities by connecting SLIET faculty members with students seeking career advice, academic support, and skill-building guidance. This dedicated mentorship website enables students to book one-on-one sessions with faculty mentors based on their availability, fostering smooth communication and personalized guidance within the SLIET community. Through this initiative, we aim to strengthen student-faculty interactions, providing continuous support and enhancing the personal and professional growth of our students. Our commitment to mentorship ensures that every student at SLIET has access to the resources and expertise they need to succeed and thrive in their chosen paths.
